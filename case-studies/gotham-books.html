The Problem

Paid traffic was being generated, but the funnel needed stronger alignment between ad messaging, landing page experience, and follow-up support to improve lead quality and business results.

  • Paid traffic and landing page experience were not fully aligned.
  • Creative iteration needed to better support conversion intent.
  • Lead flow needed stronger support beyond the ad click.
  • Revenue efficiency depended on better full-funnel coordination.

Diagnosis

The challenge was not just campaign delivery. It was the consistency of the funnel. Better results required stronger alignment from traffic acquisition through landing page experience and into follow-up handling.

Funnel mismatch Landing page friction Creative refinement need Follow-up dependency

The System Fix

The work focused on improving not only the paid campaign itself, but also the conversion path around it so leads could move through the funnel with less friction and stronger commercial intent.

1. Paid Campaign Management

Managed paid acquisition to improve lead generation efficiency while supporting more qualified traffic entering the funnel.

2. Landing Page Alignment

Improved alignment between ad messaging and landing page experience so users were more likely to continue toward the intended action.

3. Creative Iteration

Supported performance through creative refinement designed to improve conversion relevance and better match audience expectations.

4. CRM Workflow Support

Helped support the post-click process so leads could be handled more effectively after acquisition, improving downstream revenue contribution.

Execution

  • Managed paid acquisition campaigns focused on lead generation.
  • Improved landing page alignment with ad intent.
  • Supported creative testing and iteration.
  • Helped strengthen funnel continuity beyond the click.
  • Supported CRM workflow considerations for better lead handling.
  • Improved overall conversion-path cohesion.

Results

By improving the connection between paid traffic, conversion experience, and follow-up support, the funnel produced stronger lead volume and measurable revenue contribution.

  • 3,000+ qualified leads
  • $120K+ revenue contribution
  • Stronger full-funnel alignment
  • Better support for conversion follow-through

Key Insight

Paid media performs better when the landing page and follow-up system are working with the campaign, not against it. Better lead generation came from improving the full conversion path, not just the ad delivery layer.
