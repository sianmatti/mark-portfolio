Overview

Gotham Books Inc. needed to generate a consistent flow of qualified leads while improving how those leads converted into actual revenue.

Campaigns were active, but performance depended too heavily on volume instead of quality. The system was bringing in leads, but not all of them were converting efficiently.

The Problem

Lead generation was happening, but the system behind it was not optimized for conversion.

  • Traffic quality was inconsistent
  • Landing pages were not fully aligned with ad messaging
  • Creative direction lacked structured iteration
  • Follow-up and CRM flow needed better support

This created friction between acquisition and actual revenue.

Diagnosis

The entire acquisition-to-conversion system needed alignment.

Traffic was being generated, but not all leads were high intent. The landing experience did not fully support conversion, and the post-click flow was not optimized to capture value.

Without alignment across these stages, scaling lead volume alone would not improve results.

Strategy

The focus was to improve lead quality and conversion flow, not just increase volume.

  • Align ads → landing page → offer
  • Improve traffic quality
  • Establish creative iteration cycles
  • Support CRM and follow-up flow

Execution

1. Paid Campaign Optimization

Campaigns were managed to improve audience targeting, refine messaging based on response, and focus more heavily on higher-quality traffic segments.

2. Landing Page Alignment

Landing pages were adjusted to better match ad messaging, reduce conversion friction, and improve clarity of the offer after the click.

3. Creative Iteration

Instead of relying on static creatives, messaging angles and creative variations were tested and refined so stronger concepts could be scaled.

4. Funnel & CRM Support

Beyond lead capture, the flow after the click was supported to reduce drop-offs and improve how leads moved toward revenue.

Results

  • 3,000+ leads generated
  • $120K+ in revenue contribution
  • Improved lead quality and conversion consistency
  • Stronger connection between paid traffic and actual sales

Key Takeaway

The growth did not come from increasing lead volume alone. It came from improving the entire conversion system: stronger traffic quality, better landing page alignment, and more effective follow-up.

When those layers are aligned, paid media stops being just a traffic source and becomes a revenue driver.
