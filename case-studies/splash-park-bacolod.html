Overview

Goal: Increase event turnout while improving acquisition efficiency.

Industry: Attractions / Leisure

Platforms: Meta Ads, GA4

Scope: Audience structure, retargeting setup, promo testing support, and reporting visibility.

Challenge

Meta Ads performance was limited by weak segmentation, unclear retargeting structure, and poor visibility into campaign performance. The account needed stronger audience logic and better measurement to improve efficiency.

What I changed

Structured prospecting and retargeting campaigns around funnel stage.
Built 7-day, 14-day, and 30-day remarketing windows.
Improved reporting visibility to make performance easier to evaluate.
Supported audience refinement and promo testing to improve acquisition efficiency.

Results

+35%

Performance improved because targeting, retargeting, and reporting were more aligned. That made the campaign easier to optimize while increasing turnout at a lower acquisition cost.

Takeaway

Cleaner audience structure and stronger retargeting logic can materially improve event-driven campaigns without relying only on higher spend.
