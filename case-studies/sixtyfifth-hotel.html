The Problem

Search traffic was reaching the property, but campaign performance needed stronger structure and better keyword control to improve booking quality and cost efficiency.

  • Brand and non-brand traffic were not clearly structured for performance control.
  • Keyword intent needed refinement to better match the booking journey.
  • Negative keyword cleanup opportunities were limiting efficiency.
  • Paid traffic quality needed stronger alignment with direct booking goals.

Diagnosis

The account did not need more traffic at any cost. It needed better traffic quality. A cleaner search structure was necessary to better separate intent, reduce waste, and support more efficient booking acquisition.

Intent mismatch Keyword waste Structure gap Efficiency issue

The System Fix

The work focused on restructuring the account to improve booking intent alignment, reduce low-value traffic, and support stronger optimization decisions from cleaner search data.

1. Brand vs Non-Brand Structure

Reorganized campaigns to separate branded demand from broader search acquisition, making performance easier to evaluate and budgets easier to control.

2. Keyword Intent Refinement

Improved keyword direction so search traffic would align more closely with users who were more likely to move toward direct booking.

3. Negative Keyword Cleanup

Reduced waste by tightening exclusions and removing low-value search traffic that did not match the property’s booking goals.

4. Retargeting Support

Supported the search strategy with remarketing logic to stay connected to higher-intent users after initial site visits.

Execution

  • Restructured campaigns into clearer brand and non-brand groups.
  • Refined keyword targeting around higher booking intent.
  • Cleaned up negative keywords to reduce wasted clicks.
  • Supported remarketing flow for warmer traffic.
  • Improved visibility into search performance through cleaner reporting support.
  • Aligned campaign logic more closely with the booking journey.

Results

With cleaner search structure and stronger keyword alignment, the account produced better booking outcomes with improved traffic efficiency.

  • +20% increase in direct bookings
  • -30% reduction in CPC
  • Improved traffic quality from stronger intent alignment
  • Clearer search campaign decision-making

Key Insight

Search performance improves faster when the account is structured around intent, not just keywords. Cleaner separation between traffic types made spend more efficient and booking-focused optimization easier to manage.
