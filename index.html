Case Studies

Case Study 01 — Splash Park Bacolod

Goal: Increase event turnout while improving acquisition efficiency.

Challenge: Needed stronger audience structure, better promo testing, and better tracking visibility to improve turnout without raising costs.

What I did: audience segmentation, prospecting vs retargeting system, remarketing windows (7/14/30 days), promo testing, GA4 event tracking + dashboard reporting.

Result: +35% event turnout, -25% CPA.

Tools: Meta Ads Manager • GA4 • Reporting Dashboards

Takeaway: improving scale + efficiency via audience strategy, retargeting logic, and performance tracking.

Case Study 02 — SixtyFifth Hotel

Goal: Increase direct bookings and improve paid traffic quality.

Challenge: Needed better campaign structure, more relevant targeting, and better alignment between paid traffic and booking intent.

What I did: brand/non-brand structure, targeting + negatives, ad relevance, remarketing journey, GA4 tracking + dashboard reporting.

Result: +20% direct bookings, -30% CPC.

Tools: Google Ads • Meta Support • GA4 • Reporting Dashboards

Takeaway: strength in restructuring, search efficiency, and remarketing tied to booking outcomes.

Case Study 03 — Gotham Books Inc.

Goal: Drive qualified leads and revenue through integrated digital marketing.

Challenge: Needed stronger performance across the funnel (traffic → landing page → follow-up).

What I did: managed lead gen campaigns, supported SEO/landing pages, improved messaging alignment, creative iteration, reporting workflows, CRM nurture support.

Result: 3,000+ leads and $120K+ revenue, ~30% conversion improvement.

Tools: Google Ads • Meta Ads • Landing Pages • CRM • Reporting Tools

Takeaway: support across acquisition + landing page + follow-up, not just setup.
